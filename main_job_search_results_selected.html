Job Role

Key Responsibilities

Although based in the UK this role will be supporting managers and People Business Partners within all global areas of the business. Duties include:

Advising on, tracking and managing long-term absence
Advising on, tracking and managing employee relations issues, disputes, disciplinaries, grievances, competency issues, appeals and complaints
Supporting with performance management
Conducting exit interviews and providing feedback to People Business Partners
Tracking and managing of employee compliance
Advising on and supporting initiatives to improve employee relations, retention and morale
Escalate complex or high risk employee issues to People Business Partners, as required
Working alongside the People Business Partners to coordinate global mobility and global travel activities, ensuring paperwork is completed in line with legal requirements for visas, work permits or similar documents. Including the update of information held by government or official agencies and renewals.
Liaising with Reward with regard to any health and wellbeing initiatives, as required.
Supporting BPs and CPO with policy creation and updates, as required
Any other duties as requested by the Head of People Operations commensurate with the role

Key Requirements:

Proficient with Microsoft Office, in particular Word, Excel & PowerPoint
Good communication, coaching and influencing skills
Flexible attitude
Possess high levels of confidentiality
Good attention to detail and accuracy
Good, up-to-date understanding of HR and employee relations
An understanding of the impact of change on employees
CIPD or equivalent experience
Company Info

A private equity backed organisation delivering enviable growth in its defined sector in the UK and globally
Part of a global business with operating divisions in Netherlands, Belgium, South Africa, USA, Canada, Australia and Kenya as well as the UK and Ireland
